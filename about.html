Who am I?

Hi, I'm John Foster. I'm 26 years old, born in Atlanta Georgia, and lived here my entire life.

I'm a software engineer by trade, but I'm also a big history buff, gamer, and runner!

Scouting

As I was growing up, I spent a lot of time in Boy Scouts. I started as a Cub scout in kindergarten, and was involved until I aged out as a Venture Scout in 2020

I earned my Eagle Scout award in 2014, completing a project where I built 'Friendship Benches' for Clark Creek Elementary School. These benches were to support their anti-bullying program.

I also joined the 'Order of the Arrow' in 2015, which is a national honor society for scouts who best exemplify the Scout Oath and Law in their daily lives.
